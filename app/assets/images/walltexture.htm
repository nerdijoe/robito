55 Small & Lite Background Images for Website You Can Repeat

If the background image of your site is big they add something extra to your page load time, so its always a good idea to use as small image as possible because you can repeat it with CSS. If you are not familiar with CSS but using a WordPress theme which supports background like Supernova, you can save the tiny image from the right and upload it to your site. So here some background images, I collected and created over time and thought , it would be useful for everyone. Some of them are not even 1Kb  however  look perfect when used.
